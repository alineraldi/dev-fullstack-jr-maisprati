3 Curiosities about Saturn

  • Spectacular Rings: Saturn's rings are the largest and most complex in the solar system, made of ice and rock particles.
  • Fastest Winds: Winds on Saturn can reach speeds of up to 1,800 kilometers per hour (1,118 mph).
  • Floating Planet: Saturn is so light for its size that it would float in water, as it's mostly made of hydrogen and helium.
